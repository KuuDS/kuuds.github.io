centos7下使用systemd注册服务

编辑`/etc/systemd/system/daemontools.service`

````
[Unit]
Description=daemontools Start supervise
After=getty.target
 
[Service]
Type=simple
User=root
Group=root
Restart=always
ExecStart=/command/svscanboot /dev/ttyS0
TimeoutSec=0
 
[Install]
WantedBy=multi-user.target
````
